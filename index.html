Marceline The Vampire Queen

She is a 1000 year-old vampire. Who is also the main protagonist in the series "Adventure Time". She, also, plays an electric bass shaped as a battle axe.

Marceline is also in a romantic relationship with Princess Bubblegum or, also known as Bonnibel and Bonnie. She survives her days hiding from the sun and drinks anything with the shade of red, since she does not consume blood.

Jake the Dog

Jake is a magical dog with powers. These powers allow him to shape shift and become stretchy. He is Finn's best friend and adoptive brother. He is, also, one of the main characters in the show.

He and his best friend go on in many adventures together in the land of "Ooo", throughout the series. Jake, also, has a passion for playing the violin. Later on, he gets married to Lady Unicorn and has five pups with her, who also have their own set of magical powers.

Princess Bonnibel Bubblegum

She is the princess and ruler of the Candy Kingdam in the land of "Ooo". Although, later on, she will no longer be the ruler. She is mostly known for her genius brain. She is, also, one of the main protagonist in the series.

She is completely maid out of bubblegum, hence the name. She is also called "Bonnie" and is also in a relationship with Marceline The Vampire Queen.

Finn the Human

Finn is the main protagonist of the series. He and his best friend and adoptive brother, Jake the dog, have many adventures throughout the land of "Ooo". He has no magical powers, unlike Jake, since he is only human.
